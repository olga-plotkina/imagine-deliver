New Majority Solutions

Our work delivers the best insights, strategy, and design by applying a targeted universalism approach to activate the solutions of people experiencing a problem firsthand. Our model, User as Designer®, elevates overlooked innovators and places them at the center as the creative driving force for elegant, sustainable, and actionable solutions.

Justice is the Way to Innovation

Embracing a bias towards justice is crucial when it comes to analyzing community data. Institutions and organizations can’t innovate until they have built a foundation of justice in their strategy. Committing to bold new ideas, means your analysis needs a non-neutral or justice lens that centers the needs and experiences of people whose voices have been historically excluded, uninvited, or ignored.

Nothing Sits on a Shelf

An actionable strategy makes implementing insights irresistible because the logic is sound and the next step is clear. We tailor our approach in a way that not only meets your goals & objectives and provides the desired deliverables, but also infuses the whole process with community voice and end-user lived experience. This path will produce a plan that does not sit on a shelf, but rather one that becomes an active daily guide over the next five years.
